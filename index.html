Mass Shootings Across The United States of America

About This Map

This map was created with data from The Stanford Mass Shootings of America (MSA) project which can be found at, Stanford MSA Github repository .

In the wake of a generational pandemic the United States is beginning to get back to normal. Unfortunately this means a resumption to the epidemic of gun violence in communities across the country, particularly mass shootings. Using data collated from the Stanford Mass Shootings of America (MSA) data project this map displays publicly available data regarding the locations of mass shootings across the U.S. from 1966 onward. In this context mass shootings are defined as an instance of gun violence with three or more victims (not necessarily fatalities) not identified as being related to gang, drug or organized crime scenarios.
